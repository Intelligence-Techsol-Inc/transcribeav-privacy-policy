Privacy Notice

Audio or Video files

The core feature of the TranscribeAV app is to provide transcription of any given audio or video file. Transcription in different languages requires significant processing power and cannot be done with appropriate accuracy on a mobile device. In order to get a translation the mobile app sends the file to Apple server where the actual transcription process takes place. The process is fully automated, no humans are involved into it.

At any moment of time, you could immediately remove all your translations as well as the documents from the app and our servers by clicking the Delete account in the Settings page.

Login Account

In order to preserve the transcriptions, audio and video files among several devices you could login with your account. The sole purpose of storing your login account association is to provide you the access to previously transcribed documents on a new device.

Email

Some of the services in the app allow you to send us an email. We will use the information you provide, such as email address, only to respond to your inquiry and follow up on the TranscribeAV app related issues. Keep in mind that email transmissions are not encrypted by default, so we suggest you do not send sensitive information such as Social Security numbers, credit card numbers or bank account information.

Analytics

TranscribeAV mobile app uses third-party Firebase platform to gather mobile app usage data and technical problems information. The information TranscribeAV app and these third-party services collect includes:

  • IP address of your phone;
  • The date and time you accessed the mobile app;
  • The operating system of your device; or
  • To assist us in analyzing how our Service is used.
